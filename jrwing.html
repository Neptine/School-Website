JUNIOR WING

LKG TO CLASS I

The academic systems at Air Force School Yelahanka follows the syllabus prescribed by the Air HQ, New Delhi. Junior Wing includes LKG, UKG & 1st Standard. The teaching at Air Force School Yelahanka is student-centric, project-based, and incorporates the embedded learning concept. Students are exposed to hands-on experiences and stimulating environments to enhance their understanding of concepts. While every student follows the general curriculum, he or she can also develop talents and interests in personal areas of academic interest, and in sport, theatre, music and dance, for which facilities are outstanding. New techniques of teaching collaborated with technology provide an extremely conducive environment to the students for learning. The school follows the syllabus prescribed by the Air HQ, New Delhi. Air HQ has prescribed CBSE syllabus curriculum based on NCERT framework. Steps are taken to ensure all-round development of the children takes place.
